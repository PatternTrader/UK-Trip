Chronological UK Family Itinerary

A day-by-day plan from London to Scotland with each day's key stops listed next to a matching photo.

Dates: April 2-12, 2025 Trip Length: 11 days Family of 5

Day-by-Day Timeline

Day 1

Arrive in London

April 2
  • Fly in and check into a family hotel near South Bank.
  • Take an easy evening walk along the Thames.
  • See Tower Bridge lit up at night.
  • Have dinner near Borough Market.

Tip: Keep Day 1 light to recover from travel and jet lag.

Day 2

Classic London Icons

April 3
  • Visit the Tower of London and Crown Jewels.
  • Take a Thames river cruise toward Westminster.
  • Watch the Changing of the Guard at Buckingham Palace.
  • Relax in Hyde Park in the afternoon.

Tip: Reserve Tower of London tickets early in spring.

Day 3

Museums and Westminster

April 4
  • Tour the British Museum.
  • Walk through Westminster Abbey and Parliament area.
  • Photo stop at Big Ben.
  • Cross Millennium Bridge and visit Tate Modern.

Tip: Focus on 2-3 museum zones for kids.

Day 4

Stonehenge and White Cliffs

April 5
  • Drive to Stonehenge in the morning.
  • Continue east to the White Cliffs of Dover.
  • Walk the cliff path and enjoy Channel views.
  • Return to London in the evening.

Tip: This is easiest with a rental car.

Day 5

Stratford-upon-Avon

April 6
  • Visit Shakespeare's Birthplace.
  • Stop by Anne Hathaway's Cottage.
  • Take a River Avon walk.
  • Drive back to London for final night.

Tip: Leave early to avoid afternoon motorway traffic.

Day 6

Train to Edinburgh

April 7
  • Take the LNER train from London to Edinburgh.
  • Check into hotel near Royal Mile.
  • Explore Grassmarket and Victoria Street.
  • Enjoy a relaxed pub dinner.

Tip: Reserve seats around a table for family comfort.

Day 7

Edinburgh Castle and Old Town

April 8
  • Visit Edinburgh Castle in the morning.
  • Walk the Royal Mile toward Holyrood Palace.
  • Optional hike up Arthur's Seat.
  • Dinner in Old Town.

Tip: Book a castle slot early for shorter lines.

Day 8

Stirling Day Trip

April 9
  • Take a day trip to Stirling Castle.
  • See the Wallace Monument area.
  • Visit the Bannockburn Heritage Centre.
  • Return to Edinburgh for evening.

Tip: Stirling is quick from Edinburgh and very family friendly.

Day 9

Highlands Drive

April 10
  • Pick up rental car and drive north from Edinburgh.
  • Visit Loch Ness and Urquhart Castle.
  • Continue through Glencoe Valley.
  • Overnight in Inverness or Fort William area.

Tip: Plan buffer time for scenic stops.

Day 10

Isle of Skye

April 11
  • Drive via Eilean Donan Castle photo stop.
  • Visit Fairy Pools.
  • Hike to Old Man of Storr viewpoint.
  • Sunset at Neist Point if weather allows.

Tip: Pick 2-3 top stops to avoid rushing.

Day 11

Return and Fly Home

April 12
  • Drive back toward Edinburgh.
  • Return rental car.
  • Arrive at airport about 2.5 hours before flight.
  • Fly home.

Tip: Keep this day flexible in case of traffic or weather delays.
